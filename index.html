Common questions

Before you ask

What happens after I send an inquiry? +

We respond within two working days. The first conversation is a scoping call to understand your operation, your data, and whether Manar is the right fit. No commitment, no pitch deck.

Who owns the hardware? +

You do. Every deployment runs on hardware inside your premises, on your network, under your control. We train and deploy the models. You own the infrastructure and the data.

Which parts need connectivity? +

Inference runs entirely offline. The models process your data on-site with no cloud dependency. Connectivity is only used for periodic model updates and remote monitoring dashboards, both optional and on your terms.

Can Manar work in areas with no internet at all? +

Yes. Models are loaded onto edge hardware during deployment. Once installed, they run indefinitely without connectivity. Field technicians in locations like Balochistan can query an on-device AI assistant, run diagnostics, and receive alerts without any internet or satellite link.
